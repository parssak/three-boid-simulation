Boids Simulation

By Parssa Kyanzadeh

Made with ThreeJS.

Boids is an artificial life program, developed by Craig Reynolds in 1986, which simulates the flocking behavior of birds.

The behaviour is the resulting behaviour of three rules:

  • Separation: steer to avoid nearby agents
  • Alignment: steer to match the direction of other agents
  • Cohesion: move towards the center of all agents
